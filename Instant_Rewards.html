Instant Rewards

Yes! PetPro customers instantly get rewarded! No registration, no redemption, no complicated procedure! Enjoy amazing deals from Automotive garages, restaurants, hotels, retail stores, and other interesting establishments, for just being a PetPro customer! Isn’t that amazing?

- All you need to do is print your transaction receipt from PetPro (or show your online copy) (valid 30 days during the transaction with our partners)
- Present it to the listed partners together with a valid ID (Emirates ID / Driver’s License / Passport)
- Listed partners will just gather your transaction information from PetPro (name, mobile #, email address and receipt / invoice #)
- Start enjoying their services with discounted rates and other interesting offers

PetPro Instant Rewards is focused on benefiting Pet Parents. You, (our customers) are important to us, and this is our way to show our gratitude and thanks for your undoubting support with PetPro!
