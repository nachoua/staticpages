Delivery Policy

We deliver our Products to all Emirates in the UAE through our professional in-house logistics team.

Delivery Charges:

- Free delivery for orders above AED80
- AED15 delivery charge applies for orders below AED80

Delivery locations and schedule
- - Dubai, Sharjah & other Emirates – Within 24 to 48hrs

Delivery days / timings

- (Inclusive of Fridays)
- From 12pm to 8pm

Delivery cut off time

- Same day delivery for orders placed before 10:00 AM
- Next day delivery for orders placed 10:01 AM onwards for items available on stock

Delivery exclusions

- Not applicable
- We deliver frozen or live pet food items through are temperature-controlled delivery vehicles
