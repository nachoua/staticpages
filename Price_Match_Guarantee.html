Price Match Guarantee

​ If you find an identical product with a lower price, we will match that price and give you 5% more discount!

How do you claim your Price Match ?

- The item must be identical (i.e., size, model, quantity, brand, color).
- The item must be in-stock at the online retailer’s website and on petpro.ae at the time of the price match request.
- Customer must provide a live link to the promo or webpage that verifies the price.
- Vouchers or points cannot be used to purchase items under Price Match.

We don’t match :

- Discounted items or Items on sale.
- Bundle offers, rebates, coupons, mail-in offers, offers.
- Prices that require a minimum quantity purchase.
- Inaccurate Price sources.
